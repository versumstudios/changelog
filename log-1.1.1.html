Personal Feed

Up until today, the personalised feed was like magic. There are several things that contribute towards it (who you follow, which tokens you like, etc).

Today, we're we're adding a few options to make sure you fine tune it to your liking.

"followers" - include creations from the users you follow.
"collected" - include collected tokens from the user you follow.
"liked" - includes liked tokens from the users you follow.
"boards" - includes all tokens on the boards you follow.

Fullscreen mode

When viewing a token detail page, you can double click and be able to enter fullscreen mode. Press ESC to exit
